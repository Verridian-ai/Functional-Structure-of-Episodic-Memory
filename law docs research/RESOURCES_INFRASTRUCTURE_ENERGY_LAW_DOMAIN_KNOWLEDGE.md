# Resources, Infrastructure & Energy Law Domain Knowledge (KR)

## Document Information
- **Domain Code**: KR
- **Domain Name**: Resources, Infrastructure & Energy Law
- **Jurisdiction**: Commonwealth of Australia + State/Territory
- **Last Updated**: 2025-11-29
- **Version**: 1.0

---

## Overview
This domain encompasses the legal frameworks governing Australia's natural resources, critical infrastructure, energy sectors, and related industries. It includes mining, petroleum, water, energy, telecommunications, broadcasting, transport, aviation, maritime, construction, infrastructure projects, and agriculture. These areas are characterized by concurrent Commonwealth and State/Territory jurisdiction, complex regulatory frameworks, and specialized tribunals.
